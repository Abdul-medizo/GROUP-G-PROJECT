Motor Bike Accident on the Increase Again!

Accidents are on the on the increase within and around the Mile 91 municipality Quite recently, dozens of motor bike accidents have been reported in the various hospital in Mile 91. In a recent visit to the Emergency hospital in the western part of the city. As the case has always been critical road accidents ranging from broken legs to head injury are frequently being referred to the emergency Hospital for proper medical attention.

Today in Mile 91 people see okada transport as a faster means to get to their various destinations on time

some may agree that it is a suitable means to get to one destination, but taking cognizance the narrow road network and the congested traffic around town there is all possibility for accident to come your way.
Therefore we the citizen of this country is calling on Papa Government through its Traffic Division to put stringent measures in place to help curb this okada problem before it claim the lives of other Sierra Leoneans.

Safety Concerns About Bike Riders

The people have demonstrated their need for such means of transportation, but the lack of law enforcement to further control these okada riders poses a very serious threat to our nation safety, It is believed that while many of the owners of the okadas are amassing themselves with the money generated by their motor cycles, the have deliberately decided to pay deaf ears and blind eyes to the money safety and social problems these riders pose to our people.

Solution to the Problem

o Police Officers should be barred from owing commercial motorcycles o Authorities must take full control of all matters relating to the planning, regulating and managing the entire road transport system

o To control traffic and save lives of both okada passengers o No Okada riders must ride against the direction prohibited by traffic laws. o Riding an Okada without a crash helmet is not only punishable by fines; but that the rider will lose his/her bike.
